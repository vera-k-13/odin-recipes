Boiled Eggs

Description

Those eggs are perfect for breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • some salt

Steps

  1. Boil some water in a pot
  2. Put the eggs on the pot
  3. Set a timer for 6 minutes
  4. When the timer goes off, remove the eggs from the boiling water
  5. Let the eggs sit for a few minutes
  6. Peel the eggs
  7. Add some salt
