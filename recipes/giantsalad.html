Giant Salad Recipe

Description

This crunchy chopped salad is everything you need to get all the nutrients.

Ingredients

  • 1 lettuce
  • 1 cucumber
  • 3 radishes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 avocado
  • seeds
    • sunflower seeds
    • pumpkin seeds
    • black sesame seeds
    • chia seeds
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • apple cider vinegar
  • your protein of choice (meat, eggs or fish)

Steps

  1. Wash all the vegetables
  2. Chop all the vegetables
  3. Put on your food gloves and massage everything
  4. Add a lot of seeds
  5. Add some olive oil and apple cider vinegar
  6. Eat it with your protein of choice (meat, eggs or fish)
