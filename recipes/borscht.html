Borscht

Desciption

Hot and hearty, best served with fresh sour cream and a piece of black bread. Delicious!

Ingredients

  • some meat
  • one carrot
  • one beetroot
  • four potatoes
  • one onion
  • some cabbage
  • two tbsp tomato paste
  • some salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Wash the beetroot and bake it in the oven for an hour and a half (or until tender), let cool, peel and shred using a box grater.
  2. Roast the meat for 20 minutes at 180C.
  3. Finely chop the carrot and onion.
  4. Saute the carrot and onion for about 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add the beet and cook for 2 more minutes.
  6. Add the tomato paste and cook for another 2 minutes.
  7. Peel the potato and dice into medium-sized cubes.
  8. Transfer the potatoes to a large stock pot filled with water.
  9. Add the meat.
  10. Bring to a boil and let simmer over medium heat for about 5 minutes.
  11. Add the finely shredded cabbage and keep cooking for about 15 minutes.
  12. Then add the sauteed vegetables and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes.
  13. Salt and pepper to taste.
